Improvements to slide deck on measuring risk for DeFi collateral. The deck presents a "DeFi Asset Risk Pipeline" by Amaury Denny (Ellen Capital, March 2026) — a purely quantitative, on-chain-native framework that decomposes collateral assets into dependency DAGs and scores them across five PD components (market, counterparty, smart contract, liquidity, oracle), then maps to AAA-D credit ratings. Debate what should be improved: structure, mathematical rigor, missing risks, presentation clarity, calibration approach, and whether the model's known limitations (independence assumption, no governance factor, no backtests) undermine the pitch.
