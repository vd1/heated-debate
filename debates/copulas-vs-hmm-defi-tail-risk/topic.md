Whether DeFi risk models should use copulas or regime-switching HMMs to handle correlated tail risk. Context: a DeFi asset risk pipeline currently assumes independence across five PD components (market, counterparty, smart contract, liquidity, oracle) and needs to upgrade to capture joint tail behavior.
